Carpooling

It's a fast, user-friendly and affordable way to get around Ridesharing already existed, but offers a whole new way to share your trips and travel costs.

An economical alternative to traditional carpooling services, Carpool allows you to carpool with people who are already going in the same direction as you.

Are you a driver? Submit the free spaces for your car on the Waze app. Are you a passenger? Use the Waze Carpool app to find drivers on your route.
